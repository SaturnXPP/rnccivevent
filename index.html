Core Mechanics — Click to Reveal

Land Claiming

Each team starts with 3 banner posts. Plant a banner to claim a 64×64 chunk. Banners can be destroyed in combat to unclaim land. You may claim up to 8 regions total — quality over quantity. Claimed land shows particle borders.

Resource Nodes

Five Named Resource Nodes spawn across the map: an Iron Mine, Gold Vein, Ancient Library (XP), Enchanted Grove (Silk Touch crops), and the Blacksmith Forge (faster smelting). Controlling a node grants its bonus to your entire team for as long as you hold it.

Diplomacy

Declare War, form Alliances, or sign Non-Aggression Pacts in #diplomacy chat. NAPs last minimum 30 minutes. Alliances cap at 2 kingdoms. All deals are player-enforced — admins don't referee, only moderate cheating. Betrayal is legal and encouraged.

The Obsidian Throne

The Throne spawns at 0,0 during Age of War. Any player who stands on it earns 1 point per minute for their kingdom. If your team holds the Throne uncontested for 10 consecutive minutes, you trigger the Throne Gambit — double points until contested. Final Throne holder at border closure wins.

The Crazy Kingdomz Twist

Every 90 minutes, a random Chaos Edict is drawn from the Crazy Kingdomz Deck. Edicts include: Double XP Drop, Phantom Storm (phantoms spawn for all), Treasure Hunt (chest spawns at random coords announced in chat), Lava Rising (lava level climbs 2 blocks below current sea level), and Blessing of Madness (random potion effect on all players for 10 min).

Rules & Code of Conduct
  • No hacked clients or x-ray. Baritone is banned.
  • No TNT cannoning or duping mechanics.
  • Combat logging is a 10-point deduction per offense.
  • Spawn zone (200 block radius) is a permanent no-PvP zone.
  • No griefing outside of declared war — Age of Founding and Expansion are protected.
  • Staff decisions are final. Sportsmanship matters.
  • Team cap is 6. Alliances can have at most 2 kingdoms.
  • Respawn is unlimited — no permadeath.
  • Discord must be used for all diplomatic communications.
  • Screen recording encouraged — clip your best moments!
  • Spectator mode only for eliminated players until final hour.
  • Any exploit bugs must be reported immediately to staff.
